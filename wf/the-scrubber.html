1. SOURCING & AI CHECK

üõë CANNOT PUBLISH:
  • Unsourced factual claims
  • Unnamed or unidentifiable sources

AI Language Red Flags

  • Generic hype: "miraculous," "game-changer," "cutting-edge," "tremendous success"
  • Smooth assertions without sources
  • Formulaic endings: "legacy persists," "testament to," "continues to this day"
  • Perfect narrative with no complications or contradictions
  • Paraphrasing instead of real quotes
  • Marketing/Wikipedia tone vs. journalistic voice

2. FACT-CHECK

‚úì VERIFY EVERY:
  • Name, title, spelling, credential
  • Date, time, age, timeline
  • Number, statistic, financial figure, measurement
  • Location, address, geographic detail
  • Organization name, acronym, description
  • Historical claim, technical detail

Extra Scrutiny Required For:

  • Round numbers | Superlatives ("first," "only," "best")
  • Claims that seem too perfect or advance narrative too neatly
  • Information contradicting common knowledge
  • Unverifiable details

Critical Questions

  • What's the opposing viewpoint?
  • What complications existed?
  • Who disagrees?
  • What documentation supports this?

3. AP STYLE

Element Rule Example
Dates Month day, year (spell out months) Jan. 15, 2024
Times Figures except noon/midnight 3 p.m., 10:30 a.m.
Numbers Spell one-nine, figures 10+ five, 15, 100
Ages Always figures 5-year-old, 55 years old
Money Figures with $ sign $5, $1 million
Percent Figures, spell out "percent" 15 percent
Titles Cap before name, lowercase after Mayor Jane Smith / Jane Smith, mayor
Addresses Abbreviate Ave., Blvd., St. with number 123 Main St.

Local Style

  • Cloudcroft Village Council (not Town/City Council)
  • Highway 82, U.S. 70 (not Hwy or Route)
  • Otero County
