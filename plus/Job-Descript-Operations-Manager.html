OPERATIONS MANAGER

Mountain Monthly

Position Details

Department: Operations

Reports To: Chris Hearne; accessible and responsible to all partners

Start Date: January 19, 2026

Position Overview

The Operations Manager serves as the operational backbone for Mountain Monthly, managing subscription services, financial processing, and customer communications.

Core Responsibilities

Customer Service & Communications

  • Provide responsive subscription customer service using established protocols and scripts
  • Monitor and manage dedicated email account for circulation and advertising inquiries
  • Resolve customer concerns efficiently using prepared templates, resources, and escalation procedures
  • Handle subscription renewals, billing inquiries, and service requests with professionalism

Financial Operations & Reporting

  • Process checks, deposits, and banking transactions (PO Box access provided)
  • Maintain accurate QuickBooks records for subscriptions and financial transactions
  • Coordinate invoicing and shipping logistics
  • Generate monthly Circulation Reports for management review
  • Ensure financial documentation accuracy and timeliness

Distribution & Logistics Management

  • Generate and distribute mailing labels for publication circulation
  • Manage PO Box pickup schedule and mail processing
  • Coordinate with distribution partners, including Miles interface
  • Support delivery logistics utilizing existing office infrastructure

Technology & Digital Workflow

  • Use Slack for internal team communication and coordination
  • Implement productivity tools including Granola AI
  • Maintain organized digital systems for customer data and operational workflows
  • Adapt to evolving technology needs as the publication scales

Training & Support

  • Comprehensive QuickBooks training with Laura Corder
  • Customer service shadowing and script-based training
  • Access to complete operational protocols and documentation
  • Regular feedback sessions during trial period for continuous improvement
  • Ongoing support from editorial and business partners
