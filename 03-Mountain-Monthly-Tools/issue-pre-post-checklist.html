Content Review

  • All articles run through The Scrubber for editorial analysis
  • Fact-checking completed on all claims and statistics
  • AP Style compliance verified
  • Libel risk assessment completed for sensitive stories
  • AI-generated text detection run on all submissions
  • Headlines reviewed for accuracy and clarity
  • Bylines and photo credits confirmed

Technical Checks

  • All links tested and functional, esp QR codes
  • Images optimized and properly formatted
  • Photo captions complete and accurate
  • Mobile formatting verified
  • Email template tested across platforms

Legal/Ethical

  • Source attributions verified
  • Permission obtained for all photos/graphics
  • Conflicts of interest disclosed where relevant
  • Privacy concerns addressed for sensitive stories

Final Approval

  • Co-owner review completed (Chris & Hannah)
  • Email subject line finalized
  • Send time scheduled
  • PDF subscriber email sent on day of printing

POST-PUBLICATION (After Issue is Sent)

Distribution Verification

  • Email delivery successful (check bounce rate)
  • Social media posts published (Facebook)
  • Website updated with latest content
  • Mountain Monthly Facebook page lead photo updated with image from new issue

Print Distribution

  • Issues received from printer
  • Address labels affixed to print subscriber copies
  • Print subscriptions mailed

Engagement Monitoring

  • Open rates tracked
  • Click-through rates monitored
  • Social media engagement reviewed
  • Reader comments/feedback monitored

Corrections Protocol

  • Any errors identified and logged
  • Corrections issued promptly if needed
  • Correction notices posted on website/social media

Follow-up Tasks

  • Reader questions/comments responded to
  • Story follow-ups scheduled if needed
  • Tips or leads from readers logged

Metrics Documentation

  • Subscriber count updated
  • Performance metrics recorded
  • Top-performing stories noted
